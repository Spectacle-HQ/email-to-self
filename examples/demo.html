The slow-roast guide for impatient people

By Aki Mori · 9 min read

A perfect pour-over isn't about gear. It's about geometry — the cone, the bloom, the patience to let water do what water does.

We started this guide for the friend who keeps texting at 7am: why does mine taste burnt? The honest answer is grind size, but the real answer takes a little longer.

You'll learn three things: how to bloom without channeling, why your kettle probably matters less than you think, and the only timer ratio that actually survives contact with a real morning.
